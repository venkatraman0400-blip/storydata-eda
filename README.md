## What I found

- **Quality beats size** вЂ” `OverallQual` is the #1 predictor of `SalePrice` (r в‰€ 0.79), stronger than living area
- **The neighbourhood premium** вЂ” identical houses in top vs bottom neighbourhoods differ by $150k+
- **Ames survived 2008** вЂ” sales volume dropped but prices barely flinched, thanks to Iowa State University's economic anchor
- **Two outliers are data errors** вЂ” large-area, low-price entries that must be removed before modelling
- **Log-transforming SalePrice** reveals a more honest picture of the market than the raw skewed distribution
